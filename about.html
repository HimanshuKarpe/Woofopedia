About Woofopedia

A simple, friendly place for dog lovers to learn, decide and care better.

Our Mission

To help dog parents and aspiring owners find reliable, easy-to-understand information about breeds, care and training.

Our Vision

A world where every dog is matched with the right family and receives lifelong love, care and respect.

Who We Are

Students, pet lovers and dog owners who combined technology and love for animals to build a small but meaningful project.

For Every Dog Lover

Whether you are adopting your first puppy or already live with a pack, Woofopedia is designed to make your journey easier.
