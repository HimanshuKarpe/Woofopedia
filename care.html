Dog Care & Training

Simple, practical tips to keep your dog healthy, happy and well-behaved.

Nutrition

Provide balanced meals with the right mix of proteins, fats, vitamins and minerals based on your dog‚Äôs age and size.

Exercise

Regular walks, play sessions and mental games prevent obesity and reduce destructive behavior.

Grooming

Brushing, nail trimming and baths keep the coat clean, reduce shedding and help you notice any skin issues early.

Training

Use short, positive training sessions with rewards instead of punishment to build trust and good manners.

Health Checkups

Schedule routine vet visits, vaccinations and deworming to prevent diseases and catch problems early.

Socialization

Introduce your dog to new people, pets and environments gradually to build confidence and reduce anxiety.
